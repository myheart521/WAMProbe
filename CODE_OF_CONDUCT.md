# Code of conduct

WAMProbe contributors are expected to keep discussions welcoming, technical, and respectful.
Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, publication of private information, and
deliberate disruption are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Report conduct concerns through a private
GitHub security/advisory channel when possible; do not disclose personal details publicly.

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and project-affiliated
community spaces.
